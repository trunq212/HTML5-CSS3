About Us

Webcaycanh Co., Ltd was established on October 10, 2015, with the mission of bringing green space to everyone, sharing experience and knowledge of bonsai care. Coming to Bonsai Web is not simply buying a tree, but we also teach customers how to care, how to fix when the tree encounters a bad problem, bonsai purchased at the store are warranted for 7 days. up to 30 days depending on how big or small the tree is, to make sure the product is completely disease-free before buying.

In addition, if you do not buy a tree at the store, but buy it anywhere when you have a problem, please contact us, we are ready to give free and enthusiastic advice, so that the tree can be good again.

About Bonsai Web

– Leader in the field of online bonsai market.

– The first bonsai unit has bases in 2 big cities Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh.

– Warranty after purchase.

– Professional consulting.

– Support to answer questions, care for life.

What does Web Bonsai do?

Webcaycanh Co., Ltd. does 3 main items:

– Selling Bonsai for desk, office, furniture, feng shui…

– Tree care for offices, buildings, at home.

– Design and consult the layout of trees.

During the time of operation and working, we have sold and assisted in taking care of many ornamental plants, also done many big projects for partners such as: Viettel, Mobifone, Ivy, Vietjet, Euro Window, 4* hotels Sky, cafe…
