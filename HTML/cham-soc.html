Bonsai care service

What do you get when hiring Bonsai Web to take care of?

– Periodically check the plants weekly.

– Extend the life of the tree.

– Arrange suitable trees according to living characteristics

– Keeping plants fresh include: Cleaning leaves, cleaning pots, watering, removing damaged leaves.

– Add nutrients to the soil when plants show signs of lack of nutrients.

– Replace damaged, bad trees with preferential prices.

– Low cost of care.

Some care questions are often asked by customers

Question 1: Can a dead or damaged tree be replaced?

– Rent a bonsai web site. Your tree will always be clean leaves, clean potted plants, periodically fertilize, trees are extended life. If the tree is damaged or bad, the unit that wants to replace it will receive a 20% discount on the tree fee, free planting and shipping (If using the care package without). If you use a new tree care service package, there is no additional cost.

Question 2: What is the process of taking care of plants?

– Staff at Web Bonsai will come to take care of them on a fixed day of the week, decided by the customer and agreed with Webcaycanh Co., Ltd. The staff will come to clean the leaves, water the plants, fertilize, clean the plant pots... After completing the work, the customer side will check and have a time sheet, when checking everything is ok, give the time sheet to the customer. staff Web Bonsai bookmarks.

Question 3: Does Webcaycanh issue a red invoice?

– Webcaycanh company has issued red invoices and issued them monthly, quarterly, and annually depending on the needs of customers who want to issue invoices.

Question 4: How to pay?

– Customers will pay later monthly, quarterly... Depends on the customer

Price list for bonsai care services

Small tree for table, hanging, shelf height under 40cm: 14,000 VND / 1 month / tree.

Plants for ground, under the floor over 40cm high under 1m: 49,000 VND/month/plant.

Plants for soil, under the floor more than 1m high: 59,000 VND/month/tree.

If it is an underground garden, it will be calculated according to m2 19,000 VND / 1 month / m2.

Price list of tree care services with free replacement

Small tree for table, hanging, for shelf under 40cm high: 49,000 VND/month/plant.

Plants for soil, under the floor over 40cm high under 1m: 149,000 VND/month/plant.

Plants for soil, under the floor more than 1m high: 189,000 VND/month/tree.

The basic price is that depending on the value of the tree, to know the exact price, the webcaycanh side will quote and report back to the customer.

If the number of trees is large, Webcaycan will have staff come to survey and re-quote later, with large quantities, the price will be reduced depending on the number of trees.

If the unit signs a contract for more than 1 year, it will be free to replace a new tree once at the beginning of the new year with bad and damaged trees. (not applicable to plants purchased from other units)

Notes when using tree care

– The tree will be advised to put the tree in a suitable place for the tree to live a long time, if the customer does not agree and the webcaycanh party also does not agree with the place to put the tree, the problem with the tree will be the customer's own responsibility (eg. e.g. light-loving plants to be in the dark, cool-loving plants to be in the sun, and plants for hot air conditioners...)

Do not pour chemicals, foreign substances, tea residues, cigarette butts, coffee grounds .... into the tree without the consent of Webcaycanh.
